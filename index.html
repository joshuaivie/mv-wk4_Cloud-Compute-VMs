Ilera Works in 4 Simple Steps

1. Search

It's simple. On the Ilera homepage, enter the location you would like to find a hospital in or enter the name of the hospital if you already know it. Alternatively, you can click on the search link on the header to see te full list of hospital in all locations

2. Locate

Get to see where the hospital in located and the hospital star rating by clicking the hospital profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere you are.

3. Register

Register in any hospital of your choice. Click on the register button to get the registration form. We also follow up on the registration to make sure it goes through in time.

4. Schedule

As soon as your registration is approved by the hospital, you can begin booking appointments. You can also set reminders on the inbuilt calendar system at intervals you like.
